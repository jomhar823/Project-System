Earthquake Safety Tips

  • Secure heavy objects that could fall during an earthquake.

  • Create an emergency plan and designate meeting places.

  • Prepare an earthquake emergency kit with essential supplies.

  • Know how to "Drop, Cover, and Hold On" during shaking.
