    VISION

    MISSION

    We are a highly competitive local government unit that shall be known for pioneering innovative programs that improve the quality of life of the constituents. We shall do this through:

    1. Pursuit of excellence in the local government systems.
    2. Responsiveness to the pressing needs of the municipality and embedding a strong sense of foresight in all the programs that shall be implemented.
    3. Uplifting the pride and well-being of the individuals and societies we serve.
    4. Adherence to the mandates of the national government.
